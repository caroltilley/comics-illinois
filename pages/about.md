## About Comics in Illinois
This project is created and maintained by [Carol Tilley](https://caroltilley.net), [Associate Professor, School of Information Sciences, University of Illinois](https://ischool.illinois.edu/people/carol-tilley). It is intended to showcase the rich history of comics - including authorship, production, distribution, readership, and related activities - in the state of Illinois, particularly during the 20th century. This project is a work-in-progress. Please check back for updates.
